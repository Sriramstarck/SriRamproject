C.S.E

Department of Computer Science and Engineering, was established in the year 1991 with B.Tech. (CSE) program and an intake of 40. It has grown by leaps and bounds over the last three decades with intake increased to 300 by 2016. It has started M.Tech.(CST) in the year 2006 with an take of 18 and increased to 36 by year 2011. The department was recognised as research centre by Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK) who allocates research scholars to guides recognised by the university. The department has all the necessary infrastructure, highly qualified and experienced faculty for a better teaching learning experience to students. The department has well equipped laboratories (11), research labs (3), seminar halls (2) and e-class rooms (4) and other class rooms (13). The strength of the department is the faculty with a total of 66 faculty out of which 10 are doctorates, 11 are about to finish their Ph.D., another 10 are registered for Ph.D. and the remaining have M.Tech. qualification.

E.E.E

The department of Electrical & Electronics was established in the year 1994 with an annual student intake of 60 in B.E. (EEE) course. Presently, the department offers a 4- year undergraduate program leading to B.Tech degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering of Andhra University with an annual intake of 240 students. A P.G. program M.Tech. (Power Systems and Automation) was started in the year 2010 with an intake of 18 and later in the year of 2012 the annual intake was enhanced to 24. The department has experienced faculty and excellent infrastructural facilities in terms of laboratories, space and computer systems. The Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering has been accredited TWICE by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. The Department is recognized as Centre for Research by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam and JNTU, Kakinada and ‘ONE’ of our faculty member recognized Research Co-Supervisor, he is guiding “2” research scholars registered in our research centre. The department promotes research and development in thrust areas like Power System Optimization, Power System Protection, Micro Grid, Load frequency control, Renewable energy, etc..

MECHANICAL

Mechanical Engineering Department is one of the oldest departments of the institute which was established in 1980. It is also one of the largest in terms of faculty, students, and activities, continues to lead and expand its activities in various directions. The department offers B.Tech. M.Tech. and Ph.D. programs with a wide choice of specializations, electives and research areas. The department was accredited thrice by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and was recognised as research centre by the Andhra University and Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Kakinada (JNTUK). The Department has 56 faculty members, 32 research scholars and 27 supporting staff. The department is having highly qualified and experienced faculty in all streams of Mechanical Engineering. About 20 % of the faculty members have doctoral degrees and the rest are pursuing their Ph.Ds.
